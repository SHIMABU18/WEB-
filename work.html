004 : FOOD

“饺子” is the most popular Chinese food in Japan but the other chinese food is not familiar. In this page, I picked up 馒头,月饼 and 北京烤鸭.

馒头

馒头 is nothing in the inside. Compare with 饺子,饺子 have filling and 馒头 is just like a bread.

月饼

月饼 is called "moon cake" in English. This cake is eaten when Mid Autumn Festival. The one that I ate before was filled by padding and pasty cream but it depends on community it is changed.

北京烤鸭

北京烤鸭 is peking duck in English. Peking Duck that’s been bathed in honey and sherry, air cured for hours or days, then slow roasted to produce the crisp, amber-brown skin that is its trademark. Morsels of meat and skin are rolled with scallions and spears of cucumber into a crepe-like pancake, drizzled with Hoisin sauce, and served. Then you eat it and faint with delight.

005 : TRANSPORTATION

In China,transportation systems are wide variety. In this page, discovered about 高速铁路, 自行车 and 船.

高速铁路

A high-speed train is a prime choice among all types of trains as it works at a speed of 250-300 km/h and has a high-standard service. It is also called bullet, fast, or CRH train, with the initial “G”, “D”, or “C” as its serial number.

自行车

Due to many cars are filled in the road, commuters who use car are stucked by traffic jam every morning. And government decided to provide rental bicycles. It was hit but unfortunately due to many bicycles are appeard made traffic Jam.

船

China has 110,000 kilometers of navigable rivers, streams, lakes, and canals, more than any country in the world. The Heilong Jiang; Yangtze River; Xiang River, a short branch of the Yangtze; Pearl River; Huangpu River; Lijiang River; and Xi Jiang are the main ones.The Grand Canal is the world’s longest canal at 1,794 km and serves 17 cities between Beijing and Hangzhou. It links five major rivers: the Haihe, Huaihe, Huanghe, Qiantang, and Yangtze. The construction of new railways and highways has diminished the use of China's rivers for passenger transport
